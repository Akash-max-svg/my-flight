## 🎯 HOW TO USE THE APPLICATION

### 1. **Access the Application**
- Open your browser and go to: `http://localhost:5173/`
- You'll see the login page with demo credentials displayed

### 2. **Login**
Use the demo credentials:
- **Username**: `demo`
- **Password**: `Demo123!`
- **Age**: `25`

Or create a new account by clicking "Sign Up"

### 3. **Navigate the Interface**
After login, you'll see:
- **Beautiful Carousel**: Aviation-themed hero images
- **Discount Banner**: Special offers and promotions
- **Navigation Bar**: Located below carousel with options:
  - HOME, SEARCH, FILTER, ABOUT, CONTACT
  - 🎫 My Tickets, ❌ Cancel, 📊 Dashboard, 📈 Stats, 🚪 Sign Out

### 4. **Book Flights**
- Click **SEARCH** to find flights
- Select your preferred flight
- Choose seats (no pricing displayed)
- Select payment method (Card/UPI/Net Banking)
- Complete booking with confirmation

### 5. **Manage Tickets**
- Click **🎫 My Tickets** to view all active tickets
- Each ticket shows complete flight details
- **View Details** for more information
- **Cancel Ticket** with confirmation dialog
- See 10-day guarantee countdown if applicable

### 6. **Cancel Tickets**
- From My Tickets: Click "Cancel Ticket" on any active ticket
- From Navbar: Click "❌ Cancel" to select from all bookings
- Follow the cancellation process with refund calculation
- Cancelled tickets appear in dashboard

### 7. **View Dashboard**
- Click **📊 Dashboard** for comprehensive analytics
- See booking statistics and cancellation history
- Export data if needed
- View policy information and guarantees
